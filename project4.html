Reflective Essay

Project Overview

This essay analyzes The Boat, an interactive adaptation of Nam Le’s short story, to examine how multimedia elements such as visuals, animation, audio, and interactivity transform traditional storytelling. It explores how these digital features influence reader engagement, shape meaning-making, and alter the emotional impact of the narrative compared to the original text. Additionally, the essay considers what this adaptation reveals about the evolving relationship between literature and digital media, particularly how storytelling changes when readers become more active participants in the narrative experience.
